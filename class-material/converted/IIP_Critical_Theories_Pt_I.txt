         Gendering Realism -
                 Realism provides a set of assumptions about states’ interests and
                 subsequent actions based on a masculine understanding of human nature.
                 Gendered analysis would suggest these assumptions be relaxed (leaving a
                 space to consider other potential factors).

         Case Study Example: Women’s rights as human rights/ Beijing Conference
         (1995)
