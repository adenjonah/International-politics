         Personality Traits/Experience -
                 Leaders’ personalities can influence their approach to problems in
                 International Politics.
                 Traits including stubbornness, impulsiveness, and being contemplative can
                 influence how and why leaders escalate conflict or cooperate with other
                 leaders.
                 Example: Differences in U.S. presidents and reflections in U.S. policy
