         U.S. - Afghanistan War (Key Dates) -
                 1990s - Soviet withdrawal from Afghanistan following end of Cold War
                 2001 - Al-Qaeda attacks on U.S., 2001 U.S. military engagement begins,
                 Taliban regime ends
                 2003 - NATO assumes control of ISAF forces in Afghanistan (est. 2001 -
                 2014, aid)
                 2014 - Initial U.S. troop withdrawal proposed
                 2017 - U.S. commitment continued
                 2020-2021 - U.S. troop withdrawal
                 2021 - Taliban resumes power in Afghanistan
