# U.S. Public Views of Russian Invasion of Ukraine and Biden's Response

## Key Findings

### Support Levels for U.S. Aid

- **32%** of adults believe the U.S. is providing an appropriate amount of assistance to Ukraine
- **42%** contend that the nation should be delivering more support
- **7%** assert that America is offering excessive aid

This distribution indicates that a substantial majority either supports current efforts or wants increased involvement, while concerns about overextension remain relatively limited.

### Public Sentiment

The survey demonstrates mixed but generally supportive attitudes toward U.S. involvement. While the largest single group advocates for expanded assistance, approximately one-third of respondents feel the current approach strikes the right balance.

## About the Source

Published March 15, 2022, this analysis reflects early-stage public reactions to the invasion and American policy responses.
